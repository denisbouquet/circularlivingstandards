(Transfer of personal data abroad)

During the Ethics Reporting Process, our Company obtains personal data as disclosed by the Reporting Persons via post, e-mail, or via our third-party managed Ethics Reporting line, we•speakup platform.

You may report breaches to the Ethics Reporting Line - we•speakup - of the Data Controller [[businessName]] residing at [[address]] with methods of post, e-mail, or via our third-party managed Ethics Reporting line, we•speakup platform.

The Privacy Notice for persons who would like to report a breach through any of the Ethics Reporting systems, is hereby presented to you. We kindly ask you to read it in detail.

This declaration of Explicit Consent was prepared specifically for those persons who use the WE Speak Up Platform, the infrastructure of which is provided by NAVEX Global.

I understand that by using the we•speakup platform (whose infrastructure is provided by NAVEX Global, an entity residing abroad) to report on a we•speakup case or potential breach, any personal data that I may disclose will be kept on the server(s) located in Europe and that this process involves the transfer of personal data abroad. In such a case, I hereby give my EXPLICIT CONSENT to the transfer abroad and processing of the personal data that I may disclose.

If you do not make your application anonymously, your personal data that you may have disclosed, such as contact information (Address, E-mail address, Phone number) and identity (Name and Surname) will be transferred abroad, in accordance with your explicit consent.

We would like to emphasize that your or other persons` sensitive personal data listed below, must not be included in the Ethics Reporting Notification, provided it is not material and relevant to the reported breach.

  • Race
  • Ethnic origin
  • Political opinion
  • Philosophical belief
  • Religion, sect or other beliefs
  • Appearance
  • Membership to associations, foundations or trade unions
  • Data on health or sexual life or sexual orientation
  • Criminal conviction and security measures
  • Biometric and genetic data
