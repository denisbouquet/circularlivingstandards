We want to do what is right – Our we•speakup platform helps you to your hand and voice your concerns.

We strive to maintain transparency as well as high business ethics. Our we•speakup platform provides an opportunity to communicate on suspected wrongdoings affecting people, our organisation, society or the environment.

The we•speakup platform makes wrongdoings less likely to occur in the first place and shows our commitment to operate in a fair way. You do not need proof of your suspicions, but all messages must be made in good faith.

You can raise your concern anonymously by using our platform, managed by a third-party. The we•speakup reporting channel can be reached on any device, including smartphones.

How can I report my concern?

If you have a concern or information relating to misconduct, improper circumstances or behaviour connected to Ciner GlassLtd, you can report your concern:

  • Via our we•speakup platform.
  • Email us directly at wespeakup@cinerglass.com.
  • Post to: Attention Whistleblowing Officer, 23 College Hill, London EC4R 2RP.

More about the we•speakup platform

You have access to the we•speakup platform anytime, 365 days 24/7. You can either use the we•speakup platform web form to submit your concern or you can call our we•speakup platform line.

What happens next?

Upon submission of your report you will be given a case number. Please keep the case number and password in a safe place to ensure confidentiality.

Your case number and password will be required to gain access to the system for updates.
